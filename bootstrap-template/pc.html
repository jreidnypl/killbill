Punta Cana Watersports offers sun and fun aventures on the open sea. We offer snorkeling, scuba, jet skis, speed boats, water skiing, sailing and para sailing. Also Daisy the Dolphin would love to swim with you too.

Come and meet Daisy the Dolphin. Daisy and her family loves to swim and eat with you.

Punta Cana Annual Water Rafting Competition draws rafting superstars from all over the world. The race is held on Chavon River and is a 10 mile run. It requires great technical skill to make it to the finish line.
