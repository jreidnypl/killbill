The Gershwin Hotel, painted fire-engine red and dripping with massive, white awnings and papier-mâché shaped like teardrops. Throughout the hotel your eyes will behold the original art pieces of Andy Warhol and Roy Liechtenstein. Every corner and turn will display works from modern pop artists associated with Warhol's Factory. If you are a model, the fourth floor is designed for the fashionista.

Algiers Hamma Garden Hotel sets itself apart by its vibrant design, chic interiors and curated approach to arts, all intended to attract curious and open-minded guests. Algiers Hamma Garden Hotel which is the reinterpretation of the traditional lobby concept, creates a space that welcomes social interaction with inspirations of the brand's award-winning arrival experience and coffee culture.

Hotel Palacio is a boutique hotel in Punta Cana surrounded by golden sand on the island’s eastern coast far from the beaches of all-inclusive hotels. This is a hotel where you can relax and enjoy yourself surrounded by the beauty of the Uvero Alto countryside. The hotel was recently awarded the prestigious AAA 4 Diamond Status. Peace, quiet and privacy are the hallmarks of this boutique hotel committed to sustainability and its environment. Elegant, yet discreet accommodation, luxurious amenities and a multicultural emphasis enhance every aspect of the guest experience at Hotel Palacio. And an exceptional personalized service makes this hotel the “jewel” of Punta Cana.
